About me

I believe people thrive when rules are clear, fair and humane. My work connects rigorous compliance with real human needs.

Who I am

I am a governance and compliance professional based in Rotterdam. I build due diligence systems that are simple to follow, hard to abuse and respectful to the people they serve.

Over the years I have worked across banking operations, AML and KYC, fraud prevention and claims management. The common thread is designing processes that reduce risk while improving people’s experience.

Why I studied governance and policy

Working on large scale remediation taught me how rules can help or harm. I wanted deeper tools for fair systems, so I completed a Master’s in International Governance and Policy Development at the International Institute of Social Studies, Erasmus University. My research explored political inclusion and how fair representation strengthens development outcomes.
